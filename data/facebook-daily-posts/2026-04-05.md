Most tradies set their rate years ago and never touched it.

Meanwhile materials are up 30%. Fuel is through the roof. Your overheads have doubled. But your charge-out rate is the same as 2022.

Here is the quick fix:
1. Add up every cost for the week — fuel, materials, insurance, rego, tools, subs, phone, software
2. Divide by your actual billable hours
3. Add your margin

That number is your real charge-out rate. If it is lower than what you are charging right now, you just found where your profit is leaking.

Most tradies who do this find they are undercharging by $15 to $40 per hour.
